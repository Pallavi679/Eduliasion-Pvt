Process

FORMAT

Indian National Science Olympiad and Indian National Mathematics Olympiad will be conducted in 2 rounds. The preliminary round will be held on the 15th of February 2021. The classes 1st to 10th standard students will attempt question sets of different levels of difficulty. The students that qualified the first round from each group will further compete in the final round which will be held on 5th of march 2021.

REGISTRATION

The schools will be contacted and further will be asked to provide their students with the registration form for the Olympiad. INDIAN NATIONAL SCIENCE & MATHEMATICS OLYMPIAD
1. Name, contact number, email ID and other details of each student shall be required upon registration.
2. Upon submitting the registration form, the students have to pay the exam fees to the school.
3. The registration fee for each Olympiad is Rs.175.

WHAT IT OFFERS

The aim of the Olympiad is to provide an engaging environment which evokes and enhances the understanding and knowledge of respective subject, while challenging the brightest minds around the country in a healthy competition. The Olympiad offers its participants the opportunity to explore the pragmatist in them and look beyond the conventional school curriculum.

STRUCTURE

The first round of the Olympiad consists of 50 multiple choice questions (MCQs) designed to test the student's intellectual aptitude, analytical ability and reasoning. The first round of the Olympiad will be of 60 minutes.


The final round of the Olympiad is designed to test the student's savviness and critical thinking skills through multiple choice questions (MCQs). There are 35 questions in the final round which are to be attempted in 40 minutes.
