Process

FORMAT

EduLiasion Mathematics Olympiad and EduLiasion Science Olympiad will be conducted Online. The Mock Exam for EduLiasion Mathematics Olympiad and EduLiasion Science Olympiad will be conducted as a practice session which will contain precious year Question Paper. Final Examination will be based upon the similar Format.

REGISTRATION

The schools will be contacted and further will be asked to provide their students with the registration form for the EduLiasion Mathematics Olympiad and EduLiasion Science Olympiad
1. Name, contact number, email ID and other details of each student shall be required upon registration.
2. Upon submitting the registration form, the students have to pay the exam fees to the school.
3. The registration fee for each Olympiad is Rs.170.

WHAT IT OFFERS

The aim of the Olympiad is to provide an engaging environment which evokes and enhances the understanding and knowledge of respective subject, while challenging the brightest minds around the country in a healthy competition. The Olympiad offers its participants the opportunity to explore the pragmatist in them and look beyond the conventional school curriculum.

STRUCTURE

The exam is an objective-type test having duration of 60 minutes comprising of 35 objective-type questions for classes 1 & 2 and 50 objective-type questions for classes 3 to 10.


Section-1: Logical reasoning.
Section-2: General Science.
Section-3: Achiever's section
